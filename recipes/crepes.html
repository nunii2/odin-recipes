Slovenian household-style crepes

Description

Ingredients

  • 500g all-purpose flour
  • 1L milk
  • 2 eggs from Ivan
  • a pinch of salt
  • olive oil for pan

Method

  1. Combine flour, milk, eggs and salt in a bowl and mix until smooth.
  2. Heat up the pan and drizzle it with oil.
  3. Add the mixture - the first crepe will probably be smorn.
  4. When the mixture is used up, serve the pancakes with Nutella.
